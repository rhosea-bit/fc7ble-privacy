FC7BLE Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-23 · Last updated: 2026-04-23

FC7BLE ("the app") is a configuration utility for FC7 embedded devices. It communicates with nearby FC7 hardware over Bluetooth Low Energy to read and update the device's network settings. This page describes what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what leaves your device.

TL;DR

The app does not collect analytics, does not show ads, does not use any tracking or telemetry SDKs, and does not transmit your data to us or any third party. Everything happens locally between your phone and FC7 devices you own.

Permissions the app requests, and why

  • Bluetooth (scan / connect) — to discover and communicate with FC7 devices in range. Scan results are used only to display a list of nearby FC7s within the app.
  • Approximate / precise location — required by Android for Bluetooth Low Energy scanning on Android 6 through 11. The app does not read, record, or transmit your geographic location. No location data is stored.

Data stored on your device

To streamline repeated configuration, the app caches the following locally on your device. None of it is transmitted off the device by the app:

  • A list of FC7 Bluetooth MAC addresses encountered during scans.
  • FC7 device names and Ethernet MAC addresses reported by the device.
  • Optional EMS credentials (username and password) you enter, stored per-FC7 so you do not need to re-enter them on every operation. You can clear stored credentials at any time via the "Clear Authentication" button on the device detail screen.

All of this data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

Data transmitted by the app

  • To FC7 devices you select — the app sends authentication payloads and network configuration values (IP address, netmask, gateway) over Bluetooth Low Energy to the FC7 you are configuring. This is the core function of the app.
  • To Google Play Services — the app uses Google's In-App Update API to check whether a newer version of itself is available on Google Play and, with your consent, to download and install it. This interaction is handled by Google Play Services on your device and is governed by Google's privacy policy.

The app does not communicate with any server operated by the developer.

Children's privacy

The app is a technical configuration tool intended for professional and adult use. It is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Your choices

  • You can revoke Bluetooth or location permissions at any time in Android's system settings. The app will stop working but no leftover data will be retained server-side because none exists.
  • You can clear stored credentials in-app (Clear Authentication).
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally cached data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
